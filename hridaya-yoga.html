Hridaya Yoga is the Yoga of the Spiritual Heart. 

Hridaya Yoga brings depth back to modern yoga, seamlessly blending yoga and meditation, while giving the broad vision for yoga as a spiritual practice.

In this workshop, we welcome those new to yoga and meditation, as well advanced practitioners. We will present and explore Hridaya yoga from various angles. The workshop will include:

Hridaya Hatha Yoga 

Physical yoga practice incorporating asana, pranayama, and other more advanced techniques to heal the body and mind.

Hridaya Meditation

Deep meditation practice leading to calming the mind, opening the heart, and experiencing the essence of ourselves.

Lecture and Discussion

Inpiring in-depth presentation of Hridaya yoga principles and vision, with discussion on practice and how it applies to everyday life. 

"Fuse the powers of the sacred heart with the energies of the body, and you can transform everything."

 – Pierre Teilhard de Chardin

Your practicing Hridaya Yoga will bring:

  • Better physical health
  • A calmer mind
  • increased energy and enthusiasm
  • An enhanced ability to embrace and detach from difficult emotions
  • More harmonious relationships
  • More compassion
  • A greater sense of meaning and purpose

Hridaya Yoga practice incorporates the following elements:


- Tantric Hatha Yoga: practicing asanas (postures) and pranayama (breathing exercises) using a powerful map of energy centers (chakras) for opening to energies and evoking their corresponding qualities

- Witness Consciousness: learning to rest in the background of awareness, holding any experience in love

- Self-Inquiry: the extremely powerful method (made alive by Ramana Maharshi) for recognizing the essence of ourselves

- Awakening the Heart: learning to live with an open heart, while going deeper in meditation and love

– Yoga Shikha Upanishad

YOUR GUIDE

Dmitry Zelenko sometimes feels that he has lived through several lifetimes. Originally from Belarus, with a Ph.D. in Computer Science, Dmitry worked for many years in the high-tech industry, only to embark on a new life journey as a spiritual seeker and teacher after a profound spiritual awakening.

Dmitry has been practicing yoga and meditation since 2008, traveling extensively, receiving deep transmission from the lineages of the spiritual masters Osho and Ramana Maharshi. Dmitry has studied for many years with several modern masters, and he is especially grateful to Ma Ananda Sarita for the teachings of tantra, and to Sahajananda for the teachings of Hridaya yoga and meditation.

Dmitry has been teaching meditation, yoga, and tantra internationally since 2016. He is passionate about sharing practices and understanding with his students. In his teaching he brings the unique fusion of going deep into meditation and at the same time fully embracing life in all its beauty.
